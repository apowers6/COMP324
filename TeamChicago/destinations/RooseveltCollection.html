Roosevelt collection

Located in the heart of Chicago’s sophisticated South Loop, Roosevelt Collection is an open-air, mixed-use development for the 21st century. The center is home to premium national and local retailers, 342 Class-A luxury lofts and a vibrant central park. Retailers now open: Kerasotes ShowPlace ICON Theatre, ULTA Beauty, Victoria’s Secret/PINK, H&M, Z Gallerie, White House | Black Market, LOFT, Francesca’s, The Container Store, Banana Republic, Regus, Shred415, Yoga Six, Mago Grill & Cantina, Bentley’s Pet Stuff, Northwestern Medicine, Sabon, Fleet Feet Sports, Nando’s Peri-Peri Chicken, Bridgeport Coffee, Chop Steakhouse, Massage Envy, LensCrafters, and Treasures Jewelry.
