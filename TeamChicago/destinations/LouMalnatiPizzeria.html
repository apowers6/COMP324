Lou Malnati's Pizzeria

A true Chicago classic, this top-notch chain staple “can't be beat” for its masterfully crafted deep dish pies with buttery, flaky crusts and their thin-crust is excellent as well as the plus are out-of-this-world salads. The dining areas differ by location but are generally pleasant enough, so when you need to satisfy your inner glutton, it's a quintessential pick.
