MK the Restaurant

Foodies flock to MK to taste Michael Kornick's modern American food packed with big, fun flavors: Superbly crispy pommes frites come with truffled cream for dipping, oysters get a salty, spicy boost from lamb sausage, and mustard-crusted calves' liver is paired with burnt onions, bacon, and balsamic. Pastry chef Stephanie Mazzone follows the savory stuff with playful dishes such as "peppermint patty" (chocolate-peppermint semifreddo, peppermint bark, flourless chocolate cake, chocolate-caramel sauce) and "parsnipety" (parsnip-buttermilk cake, mandarin sorbet, allspice sabayon, ginger streusel). Like the food, the stylish bi-level dining room achieves a balance between elegant and easygoing, with exposed brick, a soaring ceiling of exposed wood beams, and an always-lively crowd.
