Dusty Groove

Opening its doors in 1995, Dusty Groove stands as one of Chicago's oldest record shops. From CD's, old cassette players, and classic vinyls, a true music lover can find anything. Their online shop offers much variety too. Jazz enthusiasts love this spot, because the team is always well-versed and ready to help!


Address: 1120 N Ashland Ave, Chicago, IL 60622
Phone: (773) 342-5800
Times: Monday-Sunday 10am-8pm
