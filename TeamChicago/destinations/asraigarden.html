Shop

Asrai Garden

Walking into this dark, moody floral boutique is a breath of fresh air, literally. Far from your average florist, Elizabeth Cronin decks the space out in everything from wild flowers to extraordinary succulents, mixed in with home decor, jewelry, and apothecary goods. The shop offers truly unique arrangements for everyday and special occasions, as well as citywide delivery. If you want something far from average, look no further.
