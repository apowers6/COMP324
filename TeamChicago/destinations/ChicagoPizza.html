Chicago Pizza & Oven Grinder

The Chicago Pizza and Oven Grinder Company is not for Chicago pizza purists. If you like a strictly deep-dish diet while in the Windy City then head uptown to Geno’s or Pizzeria Due. However, if you are open to experiencing pizza in a whole new form, in a place that has been perfecting their art for nearly 40 years. It is Situated on a historic brownstone street in the Lincoln Park neighborhood of Chicago. The Chicago Pizza and Oven Grinder Company has been serving up a focused menu of pizza pot pies, grinders, and salads since 1972.
