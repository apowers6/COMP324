Dine

Ann Sathers

Ann Sather has a catering team dedicated to ensure that their “good food” philosophy is carried through their catering services and special event dining. In addition to their outstanding dine-in selections of Swedish comfort food, their catering menu includes special items like pancakes with lingonberries, meatballs, and potato pancakes with applesauce from hors d’oeuvres to entrees to dessert and an outstanding, trained staff to serve them. Additionally, their flagship location on Belmont and their cafes are available for private catered events both during the day and at night for groups of 50 or more.
