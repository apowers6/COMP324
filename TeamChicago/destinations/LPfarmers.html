Lincoln Park Farmers Market

Lincoln Park Farmers Market is a local's summer favorite. It runs from May-October, and has a boat-load of locally sourced vendors. You can grab a snack from a Chicago food truck, or buy locally sourced organic meals and ingredients. It's a win-all!

Address: 1817 N. Clark, South end of Lincoln Park, Chicago, IL 60614
Phone: (773) 880-1266
Times: Wednesdays & Sundays: 7am-1pm
May-October
Opening 2017 Day: May 6!
