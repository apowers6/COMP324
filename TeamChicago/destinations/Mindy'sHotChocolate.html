Mindy's Hot Chocolate

Mindy's Hot Chocolate is a corky native Chicago spot. With unique brunch, lunch, dinner, and dessert menus, any customer is destine to find something they like. Unbeaten reccomends the doughnuts and -of course- the classic hot chocolate options. The malted milk chocolate milkshake is also a favorite.
