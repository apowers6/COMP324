Giordano's

Giordano's Famous Stuffed Pizza has been serving its world-famous pizza since 1974. It is now internationally known as Chicago-style stuffed pizza, Giordano's has 44 locations. Giordano's has been acclaimed as "Chicago's best pizza" by NBC, CBS Chicago, New York Times, Chicago Magazine, Chicago Tribune, Chicago Eater, Home & Garden Magazine, Concierge Preferred, Huffington Post and Chicago Social Magazine. They have also been featured on Good Morning America, Windy City Live and the Today Show among others.
