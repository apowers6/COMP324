Gene & Georgetti

This venerable steakhouse, which opened in 1941, is as well aged as its prime beef. The scene is like a step back in time: Wood-paneled walls surround tables of local power brokers guzzling Martinis and pondering a menu packed with red-sauce Italian classics and not-too-pricey wines. A working fireplace heats up patrons in the winter.
