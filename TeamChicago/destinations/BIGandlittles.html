Big & Little's

Big and Little's is the favorite spot in the city for huge number of people. The food is amazing and worth every penny. The tacos are great, especially the poke and anything with egg. The service is amazing, definitely give the pork belly a try. They've opened several new locations so no one has an excuse to skip out on trying this delicious spot!

Address: 860 N Orleans St (btwn W Chestnut St & W Locust St).
Phone: 312-943-0000
Times: Mon-Fri: 11am-9pm
Sat: 12pm-9pm
