North Pond

North Pond is Situated within the grounds of Lincoln Park. at the edge of a pond overlooking the dramatic Chicago skyline, North Pond can easily claim the loveliest setting in the city. The structure that houses North Pond was originally built in 1912 as a warming shelter for ice skaters, and has been redesigned according to the Arts and Crafts tradition. To name a few seasonal tasting are Foie Gras Ginger, Crab Caramel, Scallop Mushroom.
