The Capital Grille

The Capital Grille, the experience is one of comfortable elegance. African mahogany paneling and Art Deco chandeliers provide a warm, stately setting for their nationally renowned dry-aged steaks, fresh seafood, and acclaimed world-class wines. With service, as gracious as it is attentive, they look forward to impress you. They use only the finest, freshest ingredients, masterfully prepared in recipes designed to engage all of your senses.
